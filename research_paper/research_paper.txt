# MediPredic AI: AI-Based Disease Prediction Using Machine Learning

## Abstract

Healthcare prediction systems are becoming increasingly important with the advancement of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies. This research presents MediPredic AI, a machine learning-based disease prediction system for diabetes prediction using patient medical data. The system uses Logistic Regression to analyze health parameters and predict whether a patient is diabetic or non-diabetic. The model was trained and evaluated using the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset. Experimental results show satisfactory accuracy and effectiveness in disease prediction.

---

# 1. Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is transforming healthcare by enabling early disease prediction and intelligent diagnosis systems. Diabetes is one of the most common chronic diseases worldwide. Early prediction can help reduce health risks and improve treatment planning.

MediPredic AI aims to provide an AI-powered healthcare prediction system using machine learning algorithms.
